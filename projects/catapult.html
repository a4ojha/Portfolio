Catapult

Awarded first place for in a high school physics competition for designing and engineering a 1x1 m ballista-style catapult. Leveraged an in-depth understanding of force dynamics and kinematics to calculate ideal launch trajectories and velocities, ensuring maximum projectile range. The journey to the final design involved a series of iterative prototypes, each refining the mechanism towards peak performance.
